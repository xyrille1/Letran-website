Our Story

Rooted in Faith,
Built for the Future

"Founded in 1947 to provide the children of Manaoag with Catholic education — a mission that endures today."

Colegio de San Juan de Letran – Manaoag (formerly Our Lady of Manaoag College and Holy Rosary Academy) has been forming men and women of faith, character, and competence for over seven decades. Under the inspiration of the Dominican tradition — anchored in Veritas (Truth) — we prepare students to become servant-leaders in their communities and beyond.

As part of the prestigious Letran network and the global Dominican family, we hold ourselves to the highest standards of academic excellence, spiritual formation, and community service. Our affiliation with Canvas Learning Management System empowers our educators and students with a modern, world-class learning platform.

Virgin Mother of God, Queen of the Most Holy Rosary, you have chosen to raise your throne of mercy in Manaoag, to be the beloved protectress and patron of the Province of Pangasinan, and therefrom to bestow your graces on us, your children, banished in this valley of tears.

Look down with your eyes of mercy upon me, who am beset with so many dangers of body and soul. Despise not my petition, O dearest Mother, but intercede for me before your Divine Son, now and at the hour of my death. Amen.
